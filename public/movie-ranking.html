My top 3 movies according to weak memory

Big Fish

I have watched this as a teen and thought it was clever. The way the story is told is whimsical and it's a love story that keeps on giving.

Four Rooms

Four Rooms is perhaps Tarantino's only good movie. It's a fine blend of "this is the worst movie I've ever seen" and "I've already watched this 7 times and can't get enough of it". At a certain point, you'll stop feeling sorry for the main character and start to question whether you're a diabolical piece of shit, as you begin to actually enjoy seeing the man suffer at the cost of his own arrogance.

Shrek (all of them)

This one does not need explaining, it's just very good. Way ahead of its time.
